In your absence

You have gone a long distance From our broken door, But from morning till midnight We miss you so more. When we stay alone in home Then cry for you! We close our eyes & in mind Floats your known view. Tear drops come from eyes Filling mind with pain, Memory of my little angle comes Again & again. But we become so happy Thinking your new joy, So pray to Almighty For our little toy!
